Our entry for the first official s&box Game Jam, a caveman themed survival game with combat, resource gathering, crafting and building.

I worked on the weapon and tool models, building assets, map, materials, and VFX.

The wood and stone building pieces use trimsheets and duplicated geometry in order to have one 1k material per kit.
